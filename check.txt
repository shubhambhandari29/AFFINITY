Goal 1: Production Support, Stability & Continuous Improvement
Objective:
Ensure stable, secure, and uninterrupted operation of SCC Application post-go-live with structured support and continuous enhancements.
Activities:
Provide L1/L2/L3 production support for application incidents and service requests.
Implement structured defect tracking, prioritization, root cause analysis (RCA), and resolution process.
Deliver timely bug fixes, hotfix deployments, and minor UI/UX enhancements.
Perform application health checks and regression validation testing to avoid recurrence of issues.
Monitor application performance, API response times, and database health.
Support production release management and controlled deployment activities.
Maintain logging, error monitoring, and operational dashboards for issue visibility.
Support configuration updates and environment maintenance across DEV/UAT/PROD.
Provide periodic knowledge transfer and operational documentation updates.
Conduct monthly service review meetings and backlog assessment.
Deliverable:
Stable, continuously monitored production application with defined SLAs, improved reliability, and controlled enhancement mechanisms.
